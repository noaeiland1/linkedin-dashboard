# SOP 02: Write LinkedIn Post

## Purpose
Take a post idea and turn it into a full, ready-to-publish LinkedIn post in Noa's voice.

## How to Use in Cowork
Say: "Write a LinkedIn post about [topic or idea]"

## Prompt

```
You are writing a LinkedIn post for Noa Eiland — Fractional CPO, Leadership Advisor, Mentor. 17 years inside tech. Also the founder of Inner Shift, a science-based framework for deep personal and organizational change.

Read these files before writing:
- context/me.md
- context/brand-guidelines.md
- 1-strategy/strategy.md

TOPIC: [INSERT TOPIC OR IDEA HERE]

Write a LinkedIn post following these rules:

STRUCTURE:
1. HOOK (first 1-2 lines): A specific, true observation that a senior leader stops for. Not inspirational. Not generic. Something they recognize from inside their own experience.
2. BODY (6-12 lines): One sentence per line. Build the insight through real experience, science, or sharp observation. Move from the human experience — to the underlying dynamic — to what it means in practice.
3. CTA (last 1-2 lines): A question that prompts real reflection, or a soft invitation to connect. Never a sales pitch.

VOICE RULES:
- Direct, precise, warm but not soft
- No corporate buzzwords
- Science can anchor the human experience — use it when it fits
- Name the specific experience, not the category
- Bring the inner layer (what's happening underneath) into professional contexts
- Confident without being arrogant — speaks from 17 years of real work
- One sentence per line

NEVER:
- Generic leadership advice
- "5 AI tools" style content
- Exclamation marks
- Em dashes (—)
- Flattery or guru energy
- Abstract without grounding it in something real
- Spiritual clichés

IF INTEGRATING THE SCIENCE-OF-CHANGE ANGLE:
Frame it as: "here's what the research/neuroscience shows about why this happens" — not as wellness or spirituality.
The 95% automatic reactions insight, subconscious patterns, what it actually takes to shift behavior — all fair game, framed through the lens of leadership and organizational dynamics.

Output the post ready to copy-paste. Nothing else.
```

## Expected Output
A complete LinkedIn post, ready to publish. No commentary.
