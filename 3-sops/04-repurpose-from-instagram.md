# SOP 04: Adapt Inner Shift Content for LinkedIn

## Purpose
Take a piece of Noa's Inner Shift content (Hebrew Instagram) and adapt it for LinkedIn — keeping the science and depth, reframing it for a professional leadership audience.

## How to Use in Cowork
Say: "Adapt this for LinkedIn: [paste content]"

## Prompt

```
You are adapting content from Noa Eiland's Inner Shift Instagram to LinkedIn.

Read these files first:
- context/me.md
- context/brand-guidelines.md
- 1-strategy/strategy.md

ORIGINAL CONTENT:
[PASTE HERE]

LANGUAGE TARGET: [Hebrew / English]

The Inner Shift content deals with the science of deep personal change — subconscious patterns, automatic reactions, what it takes to actually shift. For LinkedIn, reframe this through the leadership and organizational lens.

WHAT TO KEEP:
- The core scientific insight
- The specific, named experience that makes people think "that's me"
- The depth — this isn't wellness content, it's how humans actually work

WHAT TO CHANGE:
- The context: individual inner life → leader in an organization
- The examples: personal patterns → leadership patterns, team dynamics, organizational change
- The CTA: relevant to Noa's LinkedIn audience (senior leaders, founders, managers)
- Language/register: LinkedIn professional but never corporate

WHAT NOT TO DO:
- Don't strip the science — it's what makes this different
- Don't add spiritual framing or wellness language
- Don't make it generic leadership advice
- Don't lose the precision of the original insight

Return the adapted LinkedIn post, ready to publish.
```

## Expected Output
A LinkedIn-ready post that brings the Inner Shift science angle into the leadership context.
