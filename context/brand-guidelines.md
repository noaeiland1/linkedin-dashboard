# Brand Guidelines — Noa Eiland (LinkedIn)

## Tone of Voice

**Deep, not trendy.** The content goes somewhere others don't.
**Direct.** Says what needs to be said without wrapping it in corporate language.
**Precise.** Names things exactly — the specific experience, not the category.
**Worm but not soft.** There is real care for the reader. No flattery.
**Credible through experience.** Not theory. Not frameworks. What I''e actually seen and lived.
**Curious.** Brings science and human insight together without being academic.

The brand personality: the person in the room who names the thing no one else is saying — and then helps you do something about it.

## Writing Voice

### Rhythm

- One sentence per line for scannability
- Short, precise sentences mixed with moments of depth
- No walls of text
- Speaks like a real person thinking, not a consultant presenting

### Signature Patterns

- Opens with a specific, true observation that a senior leader would recognize immediately
- Moves from the human experience … all theinsight • to what it means in practice
